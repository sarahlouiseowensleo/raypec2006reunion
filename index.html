Before the Reunion

Golf and driving range

Planning to come early? Reunion attendees can receive half-price buckets of balls at the driving range with the purchase of a reunion ticket.

Classmates are welcome to golf on their own or with a group before the evening event. Rounds of golf are not included with the reunion ticket and will be at each guest’s own expense.

Please book tee times directly through the Creekmoor Golf Course Pro Shop. The reunion planning committee will not be coordinating groups, reserving tee times, or facilitating golf payments.

Course and range attire

Creekmoor requires golf attire for the course and driving range: collared shirts and no jeans.

Reunion attire

The evening event is smart casual. Jeans are welcome for the reunion itself.

Activities

A few fun extras for the night.

We’re keeping the evening relaxed and social, with a few throwback touches and optional activities throughout the night.

Class slideshow

We’ll share a slideshow during the event if we receive enough photos and updates through the questionnaire.

Throwback playlist

Expect a curated playlist of songs from our high school years to keep the nostalgia rolling.

Photo booth

Grab old friends, spouses, and classmates for new photos to add to the memory pile.

Networking table

Classmates may leave business cards or contact information for anyone interested in connecting after the event.

Late-night karaoke

For the late-night crowd, we’ll end with karaoke for anyone brave enough to claim the mic.
