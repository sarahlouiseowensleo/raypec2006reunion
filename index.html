Before the Reunion

Golf and driving range details

If you would like to come early and enjoy Creekmoor, reunion attendees can receive half-price buckets of balls at the driving range with the purchase of a reunion ticket.

Guests are also welcome to golf on their own or with a group before the event.

Golf attire is required on the course and range.

Creekmoor's golf dress code is strictly enforced for anyone golfing or using the driving range: collared shirts and no jeans.

The reunion itself is smart casual, and jeans are completely fine.

Keeping the focus on reconnecting

This event is meant to be a social gathering where everyone can catch up on the last twenty years and have some fun.

We welcome business connections, but to keep the evening focused on conversation and friendship, we will have a designated networking table where classmates may leave business cards or contact information. We kindly ask that attendees do not use the reunion to actively promote or solicit business during the event.
