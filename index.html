The Main Event

Come catch up with the Class of 2006.

Twenty years have flown by, and we hope you will join us for a fun, relaxed night together. Whether you have stayed in touch or have not seen many classmates since graduation, this reunion is a chance to share stories, reconnect, and make a few new memories.

Before the Reunion

Golf and driving range details

Come early, warm up your swing, or just enjoy Creekmoor before the main event begins.

Driving Range

Reunion attendees can receive half-price buckets of balls from 5:00 PM to 7:00 PM with the purchase of a reunion ticket.

Golf Beforehand

Guests are welcome to golf on their own or with a group before the event. Please coordinate tee times directly with Creekmoor.

Course Dress Code

Golf attire is required on the course and range. Collared shirts are required and jeans are not allowed for golf activities.

The reunion itself is smart casual, and jeans are welcome.

What to Expect

A relaxed night with plenty of throwback energy.

Photo booth

Help Us Make It Special

Submit photos or a short video update.

Please complete the pre-event questionnaire to share a few photos from high school and a few current photos of yourself, your family, career, hobbies, travels, or anything you would like to include.

If you cannot attend, we still encourage you to submit a short video, three minutes or less, updating us on your journey and sharing anything you would like to tell or wish our fellow classmates.

Complete Questionnaire

Spouses are welcome.

Spouses and significant others are welcome to attend. This is an adults-only event.

Keeping the focus on reconnecting

This event is meant to be a social gathering where everyone can catch up on the last twenty years and have some fun.

We welcome business connections, but to keep the evening focused on conversation and friendship, we will have a designated networking table where classmates may leave business cards or contact information. We kindly ask that attendees do not use the reunion to actively promote or solicit business during the event.
